About Jatayu Travels

Your trusted travel partner since 2010 — delivering comfort, safety, and unforgettable journeys.

Who We Are

Founded in Hyderabad, Jatayu Travels began as a small local transport agency and grew into one of Telangana’s most trusted travel service providers. We specialize in curated packages, custom tours, and corporate trips with modern vehicles and experienced guides.

Our mission is to make travel safe, affordable, and inspiring for everyone. We believe that every journey tells a story — and we help you write yours.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide safe, comfortable, and eco-friendly travel experiences using technology-driven solutions and professional services that exceed customer expectations.

Vision

To be the leading travel company in South India by 2030, known for innovation, reliability, and sustainable tourism practices.
